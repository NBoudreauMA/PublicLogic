A Municipal Solution to a Universal Problem

PublicLogic is practitioner‑led. Built from the grind of town administration, not a whiteboard. We standardize the logic beneath everyday municipal work so staff can move faster with clarity. People. Data. Systems.

The model was proven in Hubbardston through HubbCONNECT. From that work came VAULT™ — our framework for Verified Automation & Unified Logic for Towns. Quiet, durable, and repeatable.

VAULT™

Verified Automation & Unified Logic for Towns. A compliance backbone and workflow layer that uses what towns already own. It doesn’t replace people — it frees them to do the work residents feel.

Extended where it matters: meeting rooms (hybrid governance basics), budget templates (plain‑language, board‑ready), and form development (predictable intake that routes and retains).

One‑Page Snapshot

Problem: Disconnected tools, folders‑in‑folders, unclear retention, and staff wearing five hats. Residents wait; boards guess.
Approach: Standard patterns for intake → workflow → compliance → archive. Built on existing platforms (email, M365, forms, APIs). No rip‑and‑replace.
Proof: Hubbardston pilots across finance, clerk/licensing, DPW/facilities, and administration. Weeks, not years.

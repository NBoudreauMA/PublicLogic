VAULT

Virtual Automation + Unified Logic for Towns. A standard that turns forms, files, and approvals into a repeatable flow—from intake to archive—so your team stops rebuilding the same thing, over and over.

Universal intake

One smart form pattern. Clear fields, validation, routing rules. Residents see progress; staff see exactly what's next.

Automated workflow

Approvals, holds, loop-backs. Notifications that help, not spam. Every action leaves a clean trail.

Naming + retention that sticks

Consistent folders and filenames. Records where they belong, with sane retention out of the box.

Dashboards without drama

Real-time status and monthly snapshots for boards and managers. The point is clarity, not charts for charts' sake.

Technical notes
  • Works with common municipal stacks (Microsoft 365, SharePoint/OneDrive, Power Automate, email, APIs).
  • Designed for compliance and handoff—anyone can follow the trail.
  • Modular: start with one workflow, extend to your stack at your pace.

Built in the trenches

  • Designed by practitioners working inside town hall, not a demo lab.

  • Pilot-friendly: pick a workflow (licensing, DPW/facilities, payroll/time), ship in weeks.
